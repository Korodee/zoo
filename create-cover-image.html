📸 How to Create Your Cover Image

To create a professional cover image for social media sharing:

  1. Take a screenshot of the cover image above (the 1200x630px image with text overlay)
  2. Crop the image to exactly 1200x630 pixels (or use the full screenshot)
  3. Upload to Cloudinary or your preferred image hosting service
  4. Update the meta tags in layout.tsx with the new image URL

Alternative: You can also use online tools like Canva, Figma, or Photoshop to create a similar overlay effect with your photo and text.

Tip: The image above is optimized for social media sharing with proper contrast and readability.
